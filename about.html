About Me

Having grown up building computers and learning how DOS works, since a child I have been interested in computer science. My natural progression was to build websites at an early age, using Html and CSS. Since then I have continued this passion to branch out into full web development, with a deep passion for aesthetics and user interface, creating frameworks that can help bridge the division between interaction and functionality. Art has always been an interest of mine, and my path in web development has encouraged me to see the digital world as a new medium.
